Power Pepper Recovery

A nutrient-rich, anti-inflammatory powerhouse smoothie for post-hike revival.

Ingredients

  • 100g frozen mixed peppers
  • 1 frozen banana
  • 75g frozen mango or pineapple
  • 1 cube frozen spinach
  • 200ml cooled nettle tea
  • 1 tsp hemp protein powder
  • ½ tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp cacao powder
  • Optional: 1 medjool date (if you want more sweetness)

Instructions

  • Brew nettle tea and allow it to cool completely.
  • Combine all ingredients in your NutriBullet or high-speed blender.
  • Blend on high for 60 seconds until smooth and creamy.
  • Taste and adjust sweetness by adding the date if needed.

Nutritional Highlights

  • Calories: ~180 kcal
  • Protein: 5g
  • Carbohydrates: 32g
  • Fat: 2.5g
  • Micros: Vitamin C, Iron, Magnesium, Potassium, Beta-Carotene
  • Actions: Antioxidant, Anti-inflammatory, Muscle Recovery
