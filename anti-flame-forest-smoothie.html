Cool Citrus Fire

A zesty post-hike booster that blends citrus + frozen peppers for a potent, antioxidant-loaded refreshment.

Ingredients

  • Juiced: 2 oranges (about 150ml)
  • Juiced: 1 medium carrot
  • Juiced: ½ cucumber
  • Juiced: small chunk of fresh ginger (or ¼ tsp powdered ginger)
  • Juiced: 1 small beetroot (vacuum-packed)
  • Blended: 100g frozen mixed peppers
  • Blended: 1 cube frozen spinach
  • Optional: 1 tsp hemp protein powder or Applied Critical Greens powder

Instructions

  • Juice the oranges, carrot, cucumber, ginger, and beetroot.
  • Add the juice to your NutriBullet with the frozen peppers and spinach cube.
  • Add hemp or greens powder (if using).
  • Blend for 30 seconds until fully smooth and frothy.

Nutritional Highlights

  • Calories: ~130 kcal
  • Protein: 3g
  • Carbohydrates: 27g
  • Fat: 0.5g
  • Micros: High in Vitamin C, A, K, and Potassium
  • Actions: Anti-inflammatory, Immune-boosting, Skin health, Iron absorption support
